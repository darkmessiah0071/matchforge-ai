FAQ

Common questions.

Does MatchForge AI see my personal data?

No. All AI processing runs on your device using Gemini Nano, Chrome's built-in AI model. Your bio, interests, and any text you enter never leave your computer.

What are the technical requirements?

Chrome 138 or later, 16 GB+ RAM, and about 4 GB of free disk space for the one-time Gemini Nano model download. If your device doesn't qualify, MatchForge automatically switches to template mode — still useful, still free.

Which dating apps does it support?

Tinder, Bumble, Hinge, Match, OkCupid, POF, Zoosk, eHarmony, Badoo, Coffee Meets Bagel, LOVOO, Feeld, HER, and Grindr. More being added regularly.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, at any time. Go to Settings inside the extension and click "Manage account" to cancel through the secure ExtensionPay portal. No cancellation fees.

Does it work without the AI model?

Yes. If Gemini Nano isn't available on your device, MatchForge falls back to template-based optimization — you still get 5 bio rewrites, 10 openers, Hinge prompts, and tips, all for free.
