Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

MatchForge AI is built on a simple principle: your personal data is yours. This policy explains exactly what the extension does and doesn't do with your information. The short version is that we collect almost nothing — because we don't need to.

1. Data We Do NOT Collect

MatchForge AI does not collect, transmit, log, or store any of the following on any external server:

  • Your dating profile bio or any text you enter into the extension
  • Your name, age, location, or interests
  • Photos or images of any kind
  • Your activity on any dating app
  • Your browsing history
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or usage analytics
  • Crash reports or telemetry

2. On-Device AI Processing

All AI optimization is performed locally on your computer using Google's Gemini Nano model, which is built into Chrome 138+. Your profile text is processed entirely within your browser — it is never sent to MatchForge servers, third-party AI servers, or anywhere else.

When the Gemini Nano model is unavailable, the extension falls back to template-based optimization. This fallback also runs entirely locally — no network requests are made to process your profile text.

3. Local Storage Only

The following data is stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local. It is accessible only to you and never transmitted anywhere:

  • Optimization history — your past results, stored on your device so you can revisit them
  • Theme preference — dark or light mode
  • Daily usage count — a simple counter to enforce the free tier limit, resets at midnight
  • Payment token — an anonymous token from ExtensionPay, if you purchase a paid plan (see Section 4)
  • Install date — the date the extension was first installed, used for payment verification

4. Payments via ExtensionPay

If you choose to upgrade to a paid plan, payments are processed by ExtensionPay, which uses Stripe for payment handling.

The extension communicates with extensionpay.com only to verify whether your account is paid, using an anonymous bearer token. No personal payment information (card numbers, billing address, etc.) is stored in the extension or accessible to us. All payment data is handled exclusively by ExtensionPay and Stripe under their respective privacy policies:

  • ExtensionPay Privacy Policy
  • Stripe Privacy Policy

5. Permissions Explained

Permission Why it's needed
activeTab To detect which dating app you're currently on, so the extension can show the correct platform name and enable the Auto-fill bio feature. No browsing data is transmitted.
scripting To run a read-only content script on supported dating apps, enabling the Auto-fill bio button. The script reads text from the page only — it never modifies anything.
storage To save your optimization history, preferences, and usage count locally on your device.
clipboardWrite To copy optimized bios and opening messages to your clipboard when you click a Copy button.
alarms To check once per hour whether the date has changed, so the daily free-tier counter can be reset at midnight.

6. Host Permissions (Dating Apps)

The extension requests access to 13 dating app domains (Tinder, Bumble, Hinge, Match, OkCupid, POF, Zoosk, eHarmony, Badoo, Coffee Meets Bagel, LOVOO, Feeld, HER, Grindr) solely to run the bio auto-fill content script described above. The extension does not read, record, or transmit any data from these sites.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Google Fonts — Font files are loaded from fonts.googleapis.com when the popup is open. This is a standard web font request; no personal data is included.
  • ExtensionPay / Stripe — Only contacted if you choose to purchase a paid plan, for payment verification using an anonymous token.

No analytics services, advertising networks, crash reporters, or data brokers are used anywhere in the extension.

8. Children's Privacy

MatchForge AI is designed for adults aged 18 and over, consistent with the minimum age requirements of the dating platforms it supports. We do not knowingly process information from anyone under 18.

9. Your Rights

Because all your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it. You can clear your optimization history at any time from the History tab inside the extension. Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data from your browser.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will change. We will not make material changes without updating that date. Continued use of the extension after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
